# Leadership Notes

- The board narrative should show that Directing Business Consulting Advisory is becoming a repeatable boutique AI advisory business, not a collection of one-off projects.
- Emphasize conversion from founder-led consulting into packaged offerings: chief-of-staff systems, GTM operating cadence, and AI-enabled advisory retainers.
- Keep monthly net burn under $125k while protecting delivery quality and preserving room for selective contractor support.
- Show that pipeline quality is improving, with at least two enterprise-style advisory opportunities moving toward structured procurement.
- Highlight expansion paths inside current clients, especially where reporting, decision logs, or KPI dashboards can become recurring revenue.
- Every ask should map to an owner and to a near-term revenue, delivery, or client-confidence outcome.
