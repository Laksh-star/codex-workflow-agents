# Customer Voice Aggregator

## Buyer

Product, support, GTM leadership

## Job to be done

Convert scattered customer feedback into structured themes, evidence, and prioritization inputs.

## Inputs

- support summaries
- Slack and Teams customer discussions
- research links and interview notes
- local notes or docs

## Workflow

1. Gather recent customer signals and feedback evidence.
2. Cluster recurring themes and count frequency.
3. Separate urgent defects from strategic trends.
4. Build a theme tracker and evidence table.
5. Generate a product review deck or summary memo.

## Deliverables

- theme tracker workbook
- evidence table
- product review deck

## Differentiators

- cross-channel synthesis
- web research when freshness matters
- editable spreadsheet and deck outputs

## Success metrics

- reduces manual synthesis time
- improves signal quality in product reviews
- keeps evidence attached to the narrative
