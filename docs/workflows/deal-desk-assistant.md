# Deal Desk Assistant

## Buyer

Sales leaders, customer success, revenue operations

## Job to be done

Turn scattered account context into revenue-ready materials for QBRs, renewals, and executive reviews.

## Inputs

- Slack and Teams account threads
- call notes and renewal notes
- CRM or admin portals through `computer-use`
- existing account trackers

## Workflow

1. Collect account-specific context and upcoming milestones.
2. Pull missing details from CRM or admin interfaces.
3. Assess account health, risk, and expansion opportunities.
4. Build an account tracker and QBR-ready deck.
5. Monitor named accounts on a recurring cadence.

## Deliverables

- account brief
- renewal-risk workbook
- QBR or exec review deck

## Differentiators

- `computer-use` for UI-only revenue systems
- editable office deliverables
- recurring account monitoring

## Success metrics

- reduces prep time for customer reviews
- improves renewal-risk visibility
- supports faster account escalation
