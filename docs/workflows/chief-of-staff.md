# Personal Chief of Staff

## Buyer

Solo founders, executives, staff roles

## Job to be done

Reduce the daily overhead of triaging messages, tracking commitments, and following up across fragmented tools.

## Inputs

- Slack and Teams activity
- meeting notes
- outstanding follow-ups
- browser-based internal tools through `computer-use`

## Workflow

1. Collect recent collaboration activity and pending asks.
2. Identify commitments, deadlines, and response obligations.
3. Draft high-priority replies and summarize lower-priority items.
4. Create a follow-up queue with owners, dates, and recommended next actions.
5. Schedule recurring check-ins when the workflow should persist.

## Deliverables

- action digest
- drafted replies
- follow-up tracker
- optional summary deck or briefing note

## Differentiators

- collaboration triage and reply drafting
- automations for persistent follow-through
- `computer-use` to gather missing context from UI-only systems

## Success metrics

- saves 30 to 45 minutes per day
- reduces dropped commitments
- increases response speed on priority items
