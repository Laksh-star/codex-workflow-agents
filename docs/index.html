Business workflow demos that actually run.

What it does

The demo collects operating context, classifies the signal, synthesizes the business narrative, and emits editable deliverables instead of stopping at a text summary.

1. Collect

Pulls live Slack and GitHub context, plus curated KPI and leadership inputs.

2. Classify

Scores highlights, risks, asks, roadmap items, and consulting-specific signals.

3. Synthesize

Builds a headline, operating narrative, and decision-oriented summary.

4. Deliver

Exports an Excel pack, a PowerPoint briefing, a markdown summary, and a run report.
