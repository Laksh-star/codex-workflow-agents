# Prompt Templates

## Personal Chief of Staff

```text
Act as my chief of staff for today. Review my recent Slack and Teams activity, identify open commitments, draft the highest-priority replies, and prepare a follow-up list with owners, deadlines, and next actions. If key context only exists in a browser tool, use computer-use to retrieve it.
```

Automation prompt:

```text
Review recent collaboration activity, identify pending commitments, draft priority responses, and summarize the follow-ups that need action today.
```

## Executive Briefing Machine

```text
Build this week's executive briefing from Slack, Teams, GitHub, local notes, and our KPI sources. Use computer-use for any dashboard-only metrics, then produce an editable Excel KPI pack and an editable PowerPoint briefing deck.
```

Automation prompt:

```text
Collect the latest operating context, refresh the KPI summary, and prepare the weekly executive briefing artifacts.
```

## Daily Leadership Digest

```text
Prepare a daily leadership digest from Slack, Teams, and GitHub. Summarize progress, risks, blockers, decisions, and anything that needs escalation. Include a lightweight owner tracker if there are multiple follow-ups.
```

Automation prompt:

```text
Collect today's workstream signals, summarize the important changes, and highlight blockers, decisions, and escalations for leadership.
```

## Deal Desk Assistant

```text
Assemble an account review for this customer using Slack and Teams account chatter, meeting notes, and any CRM or admin portal context available through computer-use. Produce an editable QBR deck, a renewal-risk tracker, and a concise account brief.
```

Automation prompt:

```text
Monitor the named account for new signals, refresh the risk view, and prepare updated review materials when meaningful changes appear.
```

## Customer Voice Aggregator

```text
Aggregate customer feedback from support threads, Slack or Teams discussions, research links, and interview notes. Cluster the themes, quantify frequency, and produce an editable Excel tracker plus a product review deck.
```

Automation prompt:

```text
Collect new customer feedback signals, update the theme tracker, and summarize the most important trends and evidence for product review.
```
